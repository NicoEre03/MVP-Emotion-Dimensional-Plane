Emotion Dimensional Plane

Interactive tool for visualizing and journaling emotional states using a two-dimensional scatter plot where X-axis represents valence (unpleasant to pleasant) and Y-axis represents arousal (passive to active).

Mapping & Segmentation Tool

Personalize the emotion map, generate its geometric territories, and submit the final map for analysis.

Recording Mode

Journal your current emotional state using the energy system. Select emotions and adjust their dominance levels to capture your feelings.

Heatmap Viewer

Visualize aggregated emotional patterns by loading data from a unique link generated by the backend.
